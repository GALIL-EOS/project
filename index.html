CIVIL
ABOUT
Civil engineering is one of the oldest and most essential branches of engineering, dedicated to designing, constructing, and maintaining the infrastructure that supports modern life. It encompasses a wide range of projects, including roads, bridges, buildings, dams, water supply systems, and transportation networks.

ELECTRICAL
ABOUT
Electrical engineering is a dynamic and innovative field that focuses on the study, design, and application of electrical systems and technologies. It encompasses a wide range of areas, including power generation and distribution, electronics, telecommunications, control systems, and renewable energy solutions.

MECHANICAL
ABOUT
Mechanical engineering is a broad and versatile field of engineering that focuses on the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It combines principles of physics, mathematics, and material science to create machines, tools, and devices that enhance human life and solve real-world problems.

KFU
UNIVERSITY
Kafr El-Sheikh University, located in Kafr El-Sheikh Governorate, Egypt, is one of the country’s prominent public universities. Established in 2006, the university has rapidly grown into a hub for education, research, and innovation. Initially, it was a branch of Tanta University before becoming an independent institution, offering various undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
